About Kampung Cuisine

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Malaysia, Kampung Cuisine was born from a passion for sharing the rich, diverse flavors of traditional Malaysian cooking with food lovers around the world.

Our journey began in a small village (kampung) where recipes were passed down through generations, each dish telling a story of culture, tradition, and love.

Today, we continue that legacy by sourcing the finest ingredients and crafting authentic Malaysian dishes that transport you straight to the bustling markets and family kitchens of Malaysia.
